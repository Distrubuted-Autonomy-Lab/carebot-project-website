# Carebot FAQ

## Question 1: How does it work?

Carebot takes your question and processes it using a combination of Gemini AI and a custom-built project database.

The AI generates targeted queries to retrieve the most relevant information from the database. If your question falls outside the scope of the database, it is automatically redirected and answered directly by Gemini AI as a fallback.
